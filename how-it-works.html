Why choose this

A deeper model for people who want more than rushed sick care.

Traditional healthcare is often built around short visits, acute problems, insurance rules, and reacting after symptoms appear. That model can miss the bigger picture: how you sleep, train, think, recover, age, and feel every day.

Live Wellness takes a deeper preventive approach. We look at history, labs, imaging, lifestyle, risk factors, goals, and quality of life together. The point is not only living longer, but living healthier, happier, and with more clarity and confidence.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

How will this help me?

We use an evidence-based precision medicine approach, collecting a broad picture of your health through history, physical exam, advanced labs, screening exams, and selected imaging. The goal is to identify risks early across cardiovascular disease, metabolic dysfunction, cancer risk, neurodegenerative disease, hormones, sleep, fitness, and quality of life, then give you data and a plan to act on it.

Do you take health insurance?

Memberships are standalone direct-pay plans and do not bill health insurance. We do offer a la carte options for members and non-members. If you need specialist referrals, medication management, or care coordination, we handle that like a traditional healthcare practice.

Is this HSA/FSA eligible?

Yes. HSA/FSA cards are accepted, and superbills are available upon request.

What screening exams are done?

Our clinic is proactive about preventive exams. Depending on your needs and membership level, screening may include advanced biomarkers, hormone panels, metabolic and cardiovascular testing, body composition, sleep assessments, genetic testing, MRI or other imaging, and additional diagnostics for deeper insight.

Do I get any discounts?

Yes. Members receive discounts on aesthetics and other services depending on the membership chosen. Higher-tier plans receive larger add-on discounts.

What is physician availability?

We generally offer same-day or next-day appointments. All members receive a direct line to the physician, and some membership levels include 24/7 access.

What if I am travelling?

For national or international travel, we recommend pre-travel visits to review vaccines, medications, destination risks, and health planning. We also offer telemedicine visits during travel when appropriate.

Do you offer house calls?

Yes. House calls are included in certain membership levels and are also available as a la carte options.

Who are these memberships designed for?

They are designed for anyone who takes healthcare seriously and wants to work with a physician-led team to improve the quality of their life, not just respond to illness.

Can this replace my primary care?

For many members, yes. Memberships can cover primary care needs such as wellness exams, medication management, urgent visits, referrals, lab review, and ongoing care coordination.

Do you support functional medicine goals?

Yes. We can evaluate root causes behind fatigue, sleep issues, gut health, metabolic dysfunction, hormone concerns, inflammation, and performance goals using history, diagnostics, nutrition, lifestyle, and targeted follow-up.

Can my plan change over time?

Yes. We can upgrade or downgrade your membership as your needs change, and we may recommend a different level after your assessment if it better matches your goals.
