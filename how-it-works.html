One-time assessment

A focused deep dive when you want answers and a plan.

The one-time health assessment is designed for patients who want a comprehensive review without starting a monthly membership. We collect a deep medical history, perform a detailed physical, use advanced lab testing and preventive screening exams, then turn the findings into a clear roadmap.

Our clinic is proactive about preventive exams and provides layers of diagnostic insight - from biomarkers and advanced labs to imaging such as MRI when appropriate.

Why choose this?

The current healthcare system is often rushed, reactive, and built around treating problems after they appear. Most visits do not give enough time to understand your full history, goals, habits, risks, sleep, fitness, nutrition, hormones, or long-term quality of life.

We take a deeper preventive approach. It is not only about living longer - it is about living healthier, happier, sharper, and with more control over your future.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

How will it help me?

We use an evidence-based precision medicine approach, collecting a broad picture of your health through history, physical exam, advanced labs, diagnostics, screening exams, and selected imaging. The goal is to identify risks early and help you take control of long-term health.

Do you take health insurance?

Memberships are standalone and do not work with health insurance. We do offer a la carte options, and we can manage referrals like a traditional healthcare practice when needed.

Is this HSA/FSA eligible?

Yes. HSA/FSA cards are accepted.

Do I get discounts?

Yes. Members receive discounts on aesthetics and other services depending on the membership chosen.

What is physician availability?

We generally offer same-day or next-day appointments. Members receive a direct line to the physician, with 24/7 access included in select plans.

What if I am travelling?

We recommend pre-travel visits for national or international travel and offer telemedicine support during travel when appropriate.

Do you offer house calls?

Yes. House calls are included in certain membership levels and are also available as a la carte options.

Who are these memberships designed for?

Anyone who takes healthcare seriously and wants to work with us to improve the quality of their life.
