FAQ

Common questions

What makes Live Wellness different?

We combine concierge access, proactive primary care, longevity diagnostics, aesthetics, and a la carte services in one physician-led model.

Do I need a membership to use services?

No. Services are available to members and non-members. Members receive tier-based discounts automatically.

Can this replace primary care?

For many members, yes. Memberships can include wellness care, medication management, urgent visits, referrals, and care coordination.

Do you accept HSA/FSA?

Yes. HSA/FSA cards are accepted where eligible, and superbills are available upon request.

How quickly can I see a physician?

We generally offer same-day or next-day access, with direct physician communication included in membership plans.
