What changes by tier

More access, deeper testing, and more support as your needs grow.

Membership levels scale from virtual primary care access to in-office visits, house calls, advanced longevity labs, IV therapy, executive physicals, travel support, family coverage, and dedicated physician access.

Your plan can be upgraded or downgraded as your needs change. During your health assessment, we can help recommend the level that actually fits your goals.
