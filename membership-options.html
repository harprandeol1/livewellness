What is concierge medicine?

What's different with concierge

Concierge medicine means your physician carries a smaller panel so you get real access — not a waiting room and a rushed visit. You pay a monthly membership for proactive, relationship-based care built around prevention and longevity.

  • Direct access — reach your physician by phone, text, or secure message when you need guidance.
  • Same-day or next-day visits — appointments when you're sick or need to be seen, not weeks out.
  • Longer visits — time to discuss goals, labs, and prevention without feeling rushed.
  • Proactive prevention — annual and ongoing wellness work, not just sick visits.
  • Care coordination — referrals, specialists, and follow-up handled with you in mind.
  • Longevity focus — advanced testing and personalized plans as you move up tiers.
