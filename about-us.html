About Live Wellness

Proactive health, concierge access, and longevity care in one place.

Live Wellness is a direct-pay clinic built around relationship-based medicine — giving you more time with your physician, advanced diagnostics, and a care model focused on prevention, performance, and long-term wellbeing.

Our mission

We believe healthcare should be proactive, personal, and accessible. Live Wellness combines concierge primary care, longevity medicine, aesthetics, and urgent access so your health plan can follow you — at the clinic, at home, and wherever life takes you.

Every patient relationship starts with listening: your goals, your history, and the outcomes that matter most to you. From there, we build a plan that may include concierge medicine, one-time assessments, or targeted a la carte services.

Leadership

Live Wellness is physician-led with a focus on internal medicine, prevention, and longevity.
